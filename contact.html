Frequently Asked Questions

How do I sync Neo with Apple Health?
Neo automatically integrates with Apple Health when you grant permissions during setup. Go to Settings > Health > Data Access & Devices to manage permissions.
Is my health data secure?
Absolutely. Neo uses end-to-end encryption and stores all health data locally on your device. We never access or share your personal health information.
Can I use Neo on multiple devices?
Yes! Neo syncs seamlessly across all your Apple devices through iCloud. Start a workout on your iPhone and continue on your Apple Watch.
What iOS version do I need?
Neo requires iOS 15.0 or later for iPhone and iPad, and watchOS 8.0 or later for Apple Watch compatibility.
