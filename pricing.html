Frequently Asked Questions

Can I switch plans anytime?

Yes! You can upgrade, downgrade, or cancel your subscription at any time. Changes take effect at your next billing cycle.

Is there a free trial?

Absolutely! Pro and Elite plans come with a 7-day free trial. No credit card required to start.

What devices are supported?

Neo works on iOS, Android, Apple Watch, and syncs with popular fitness trackers like Fitbit and Garmin.

Is my data secure?

Your privacy is our priority. All data is encrypted and stored securely. We never share your personal information.

Do you offer refunds?

Yes, we offer a 30-day money-back guarantee if you're not completely satisfied with your subscription.

How does the coaching work?

Elite members get paired with certified fitness coaches for weekly 1-on-1 video sessions and ongoing chat support.
